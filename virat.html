Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, an Indian cricketer, is celebrated for his exceptional talent, determination, and leadership. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he rose through the ranks with his prodigious batting skills. Kohli's aggressive yet technically sound approach has earned him numerous records, including being one of the fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals. He captained the Indian cricket team across formats, leading with passion and intensity. Kohli's dedication to fitness sets him apart, influencing a new generation of cricketers. His charisma and commitment make him an icon, both on and off the cricket field.
